What we do

Full-scope machinery capability

From a complete engine overhaul to a single machined bolt — nine disciplines that keep marine and industrial machinery running.

01

Main & Auxiliary Engine Repair

Maintenance, troubleshooting and overhaul of propulsion and auxiliary engines to manufacturers’ requirements — inspection, component replacement, alignment and performance testing.

02

Hydraulic Systems Repair

Diagnosis, repair and restoration of marine and industrial hydraulic systems — cylinders, pumps and motors, valves, leak detection, piping and commissioning.

Learn more →
03

Gearbox Maintenance & Overhaul

Full overhaul of marine gearboxes and transmissions — disassembly and inspection, bearing and seal renewal, shaft alignment, reassembly and testing.

Learn more →
04

Seized Bolt Removal & Bolt Manufacturing

Safe extraction of seized or corroded heavy-duty bolts without damaging surrounding components; replacement bolts manufactured to specification.

05

Pneumatic Systems Installation & Repair

Installation, repair and maintenance of onboard and industrial pneumatic systems — air piping, valves, cylinders, leak detection and testing.

Pump Repair

How we work

From first call to sea trial

A disciplined workflow that keeps downtime short and quality high — on board, dockside or in our workshop.

01

Inquiry & assessment

We review the fault, drawings and history, then agree scope, timing and access — on board or remotely.

02

Planning & mobilization

Tools, parts and the right specialists are mobilized to the vessel, yard or our workshop — anywhere in the world.

03

Repair & overhaul

Disassembly, inspection, machining and reassembly to manufacturers’ requirements, with progress reported throughout.

04

Testing & handover

Function and performance testing, alignment checks and a documented handover — ready to return to service.

Who we are

One team behind two engineering companies

For more than 15 years, BME Services has provided professional mechanical, hydraulic and marine engineering services for marine and industrial projects — based in Klaipėda, with most works carried out overseas.

Our team of experienced marine engineers, hydraulic specialists and skilled technicians has completed projects in shipyards and onboard vessels across Europe and beyond. Because every hour of downtime affects vessel operations, we deliver efficient, reliable and technically sound solutions to the highest standards of quality and safety.
